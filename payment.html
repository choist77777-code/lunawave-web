Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Suno subscription?
Yes. LUNA WAVE Factory uses your Suno account to generate songs. Suno Free gives ~4–5 songs/day. For YouTube monetization, Suno's $10/mo Pro plan is required.
What if my Suno credits run out?
The Factory pauses uploads until Suno credits refresh. We respect Suno's rate limits.
Can I upload to multiple YouTube channels?
Hobby/Creator/Pro: 1 channel. Studio: multiple channels supported.
What about copyright?
All songs are generated by your Suno account, so they're yours. LUNA WAVE only orchestrates the workflow. Always comply with Suno's terms of service.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately (prorated). Downgrades take effect next billing cycle.
