FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is LunaWave Factory?
An automated music video factory. You set a few preferences once (genres, persona, image pool, Suno account, YouTube channel). LunaWave then generates lyrics → makes the song with your Suno → renders the video → uploads to YouTube. All on autopilot.
Do I need a Suno subscription?
Yes. LunaWave uses YOUR Suno account to generate songs. Suno Free gives ~4–5 songs/day. For YouTube monetization, Suno's $10/mo Pro plan is required.
Is this allowed on YouTube?
Yes — you provide curated topic pools and image libraries (human direction), Suno generates original audio, and you own everything uploaded. YouTube allows AI-generated content with human editorial direction.
How long does setup take?
About 5 minutes. After that, your channel runs hands-off until you change settings.
