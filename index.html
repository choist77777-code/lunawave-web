FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is LunaWave?
LunaWave converts AI-generated songs into polished, YouTube-ready videos — automatically. Designed for creators using Suno, Udio, or any other AI music tool.
Does it generate the songs too?
LunaWave turns existing songs into videos, not the songs themselves. We integrate with Suno so you can input titles, lyrics, and prompts directly from LunaWave and auto-download the result. You need a separate Suno account.
Do I need technical skills?
No. If you can click a button, you can use LunaWave.
What is "Luna"?
Luna is LunaWave's credit unit. Features consume Luna. Free gives you 20 Luna/day. Crescent: 50/day + 1,500/month. Half Moon: 200/day + 3,000/month. Full Moon: unlimited. Daily Luna is used before monthly.
Can I upload directly to YouTube?
Once rendering finishes, you get a ready-to-upload MP4 file plus auto-generated thumbnail, title, description, and chapter timestamps.
Refund policy?
Full refund within 14 days of purchase, as long as you haven't consumed significant Luna.
Which payment methods do you accept?
Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal — processed globally via Paddle.
